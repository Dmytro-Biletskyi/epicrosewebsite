Private GP & Specialty Clinics

Pains — DNA on prime slots, slow inbox, manual reminders, weak waitlist.

Core-4 — self-reschedule; waitlist backfill; shared inbox + SLA; no-show recovery.

Policy — service SMS/WhatsApp with explicit consent; fair deposits for prime-time.

ROI (Example) — DNA −3–5pp; FRT → <4h; touches/booking −20–30%; cost/booking −10–20%.

Dermatology & Aesthetics

Pains — self-pay variability, peak hours, missed consults, fragmented channels.

Core-4 — reminders with pay-link; self-reschedule; last-minute waitlist; VM→text drafts.

Policy — deposits for new/prime-time with 24–48h window and clear exceptions.

ROI (Example) — DNA −5–8pp; fill +3–6pp; cost/booking −15–25%.

Diagnostics (MRI/CT/US)

Pains — slot utilisation gaps, manual referral inbox, charge-lag, slow PMI cash.

Core-4 + Insurer/RCM-lite — waitlist backfill; pay-links for high-value slots; referral triage in shared inbox; same-day charge-out, daily submit, denials worklist.

ROI (Example) — fill +3–7pp; DNA −4–8pp; charge-lag <24h; DSO −7–10 days.

Psychotherapy / Telemental

Pains — high DNA, slow FRT, many manual touches.

Core-4 — compassionate reminders with self-reschedule; waitlist; no-show recovery; shared inbox + SLA.

ROI (Example) — DNA −5–8pp; FRT → <4h; touches −30–50%; fill +2–5pp.

Orthopaedics & MSK

Pains — unconfirmed slots, repeat calls, pre-op instructions lost in threads.

Core-4 — reminder checklists; self-reschedule; VM→text tasks; shared inbox triage.

ROI (Example) — DNA −3–6pp; FRT → <4h; touches −20–30%; cost/booking −10–20%.

Women’s Health & Fertility

Pains — time-sensitive windows, coordination across scans/tests, self-pay no-shows.

Core-4 — time-boxed reminders; deposits/pay-links; waitlist for critical windows; shared inbox SLAs.

ROI (Example) — DNA −4–7pp; fill +3–5pp; charge-lag <24h where relevant.
