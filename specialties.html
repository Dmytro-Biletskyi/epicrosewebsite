Private GP & Specialty Clinics

Pains

DNA on prime slots, slow inbox, manual reminders and weak waitlist.

Core-4

Self-reschedule, waitlist backfill, shared inbox with SLA, no-show recovery.

Policy

Service SMS/WhatsApp with explicit consent; fair deposits for prime-time.

Outcomes (Example)

DNA −3–5pp; FRT → <4h; touches/booking −20–30%; cost/booking −10–20%.

Indicative monthly value

1,200 visits/month at £60–£100 per visit, ΔDNA+ΔFill ≈ 6% → ~£5.1k–£8.0k/mo (incl. ~£0.8k admin hours saved).

Dermatology & Aesthetics

Pains

Self-pay variability, peak hours, missed consults, fragmented channels.

Core-4

Reminders with pay-link, self-reschedule, last-minute waitlist, voicemail→text drafts.

Policy

Deposits for new/prime-time with a 24–48h window and clear exceptions.

Outcomes (Example)

DNA −5–8pp; fill +3–6pp; cost/booking −15–25%.

Indicative monthly value

800 visits/month at £120–£200 per visit, ΔDNA+ΔFill ≈ 10% → ~£10.1k–£16.5k/mo.

Diagnostics (MRI/CT/US)

Pains

Slot utilisation gaps, manual referral inbox, charge-lag, slow PMI cash.

Core-4 + Insurer/RCM-lite

Waitlist backfill, pay-links for high-value slots, referral triage in shared inbox, same-day charge-out, daily submit, denials worklist.

Outcomes (Example)

Fill +3–7pp; DNA −4–8pp; FRT → <4h; charge-lag <24h; DSO −7–10 days.

Indicative monthly value

600 scans/month at £250–£350 per scan, ΔDNA+ΔFill ≈ 11% → ~£16.9k–£23.5k/mo.

Cash-flow (Example)

At £150k PMI billings/month and ΔDSO 7–10 days → ~£35k–£50k earlier cash.

Psychotherapy / Telemental

Pains

High DNA, slow FRT, many manual touches.

Core-4

Compassionate reminders with self-reschedule, waitlist, no-show recovery, shared inbox + SLA.

Outcomes (Example)

DNA −5–8pp; FRT → <4h; touches −30–50%; fill +2–5pp.

Indicative monthly value

900 visits/month at £100 per visit, ΔDNA+ΔFill ≈ 4% → ~£4.2k/mo.

Orthopaedics & MSK

Pains

Unconfirmed slots, repeat calls, pre-op instructions lost in threads.

Core-4

Reminder checklists, self-reschedule, voicemail→text tasks, shared inbox triage.

Outcomes (Example)

DNA −3–6pp; FRT → <4h; touches −20–30%; cost/booking −10–20%.

Indicative monthly value

700 visits/month at £120–£200 per visit, ΔDNA+ΔFill ≈ 7% → ~£6.3k–£10.3k/mo.

Women’s Health & Fertility

Pains

Time-sensitive windows, coordination across scans/tests, self-pay no-shows.

Core-4

Time-boxed reminders, deposits/pay-links, waitlist for critical windows, shared inbox SLAs.

Outcomes (Example)

DNA −4–7pp; fill +3–5pp; charge-lag <24h where relevant.

Indicative monthly value

500 visits/month at £200–£400 per visit, ΔDNA+ΔFill ≈ 9% → ~£9.3k–£18.3k/mo.
