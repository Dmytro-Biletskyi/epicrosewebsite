Message samples

SMS (≤160 chars)

  • Reminder + confirm — “Reminder: [DATE TIME]. Reply C to confirm or use your link to change time: [LINK]. Thanks, [ORG]. Opt out: STOP.”
  • Reminder + self reschedule — “Your appointment [DATE TIME]. Need a different time? Tap: [LINK]. For help reply HELP. [ORG]. Opt out: STOP.”
  • Waitlist offer — “A sooner slot is available: [DATE TIME]. Reply YES to take it, or ignore to keep your current time. [ORG]. Opt out: STOP.”
  • No-show recovery — “We did not see you today. Rebook here: [LINK]. If this was an error, reply HELP. [ORG]. Opt out: STOP.”
  • Deposit (prime-time/new) — “To secure [DATE TIME], please pay a small deposit here: [PAYLINK]. Free to change until [24/48]h prior. [ORG]. Opt out: STOP.”

Email

  • Confirmation — Subject: Your appointment on [DATE]. Body confirms and shares reschedule link.
  • Reminder — Subject: Quick reminder — [DATE TIME]. Confirms or moves via link.
  • Waitlist auto-offer — Subject: An earlier slot is available. “First come, first served.”
  • No-show recovery — Subject: Rebook your appointment. Includes link and “reply if mistake”.
  • Deposit request — Subject: Secure your appointment. Includes pay-link and fair window.

Policy snippets

PECR consent (service messages)

“I agree to receive service messages (SMS/WhatsApp/email) about my appointments, scheduling and payments. These are not marketing messages. I can opt out at any time by replying STOP or via the link provided.”

No-PHI disclaimer

“EpicRose operates at the administrative layer only. We do not collect, process or store personal health information (PHI).”

Data minimisation

“We process only contact and scheduling data needed to deliver service messages and booking changes. Data is retained only as long as necessary for that purpose.”

Opt-out

“You can opt out of service messages at any time. This may affect our ability to remind you or offer earlier slots.”
