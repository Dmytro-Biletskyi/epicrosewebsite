Value propositions

No-EHR speed

Deploy on top of your PMS with service SMS/WhatsApp, shared inbox, forms, and pay-links.

Weekly measurable KPIs

TAT/FRT, DNA%, touches per booking, fill-rate; charge-lag/DSO when insurer track is active.

Reversible changes

Small, low-risk steps with one-click rollback.

Managed service

SOPs, templates, weekly reviews; not “another tool”.

How it works (4 steps)

1. Assess

Confirm scope, owners, SLAs; capture four weeks of baseline KPIs.

2. Configure

Enable reminders with self-reschedule, waitlist backfill, shared inbox, and VM→transcription.

3. Launch

Go live in 10 days; run service messages and pay-links with consent.

4. Optimise

Weekly review (WBR), A/B copy, and rules to hit the KPI targets. Then branch: Self-pay or Insurer/RCM-lite by payer mix (PMI ≥30%) or presence of pre-authorisations.

Core-4 in plain words

Reminders + self-reschedule + waitlist

Patients confirm or move slots in two clicks; empty slots go to a waitlist.

Shared inbox + SLA

One queue for email + SMS/WhatsApp with tags, macros, first-response targets.

No-show recovery

Automatic follow-ups after a missed appointment with a reschedule link.

Voicemail → transcription → draft

Missed calls become text tasks with suggested replies.

If insurer context: we enable RCM-lite in pilot: same-day charge-out, eligibility/pre-auth inbox, daily submit, denials worklist. No EHR. No PHI.

Mini-ROI copy

Rule-of-thumb per month: Effect ≈ V × R × (ΔDNA + ΔFill) + V × (saved minutes/booking ÷ 60) × £/admin hour.
