30-day pilot, week-by-week

Days 1–2 — Kick-off & baselines

Confirm owners for inbox, reminders/waitlist, no-show recovery, and charge-day end (if insurer track). Lock four SLAs; gather four weeks of KPIs.

Days 3–4 — Shared inbox + SLAs

Unified queue, tags, macros, call-back list. SLA report: first response ≤4h in business hours; missed-call call-back rules.

Days 5–6 — Reminders + self-reschedule + waitlist

Service SMS/WhatsApp/email with consent; secure links; A/B copy and timing. Waitlist auto-offers for prime-time and late cancellations.

Day 7 — No-show recovery

Automated sequence with 2–3 nudges and a reschedule link; optional deposit trigger for prime-time.

Day 8 — Voicemail → transcription → draft

VM to tasks with suggested replies; reduce manual handling.

Day 9 — Dashboard & training

Live KPI board; 45–60 min team training; handover of SOPs and templates.

Day 10 — First WBR

Weekly Business Review; adjust copy, timing, and rules to hit targets. Branch:
• Self-pay: pay-links, no-show recovery sequences.
• Insurer/RCM-lite: eligibility/pre-auth inbox (SLA 24h), same-day charge-out (D0@18:00), daily submit, denials worklist.

What we don’t do in the pilot

  • No EHR integration or migration. No clinical content or clinical workflows. No PHI. Operational admin only.
