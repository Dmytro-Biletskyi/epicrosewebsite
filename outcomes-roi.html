Outcomes & ROI in 30–45 days

Every pilot starts with a four-week baseline, then we show weekly movement on the metrics that matter: FRT/TAT, DNA%, touches per booking, fill-rate and cost per booking. Insurer clinics add charge-lag and DSO without needing an EHR integration.

KPI definitions

FRT / TAT

First response time / turnaround time for inbound enquiries. Target: ≤4h in-business.

DNA%

No-show rate. Measured weekly with breakdown by clinic, clinician and slot type.

Touches per booking

Manual contacts from enquiry to attended visit. Drops as templates, macros and self-reschedule go live.

Fill-rate & cost per booking

Prime-slot utilisation and admin cost per attended visit, captured before and after the pilot.

Charge-lag & DSO (insurer)

If PMI ≥30% or pre-auths present: encounter→charge lag and Days Sales Outstanding with daily reporting.

How charge-lag & DSO move (Insurer / RCM-lite)

RCM-lite toolkit

  • Eligibility / pre-auth inbox with SLA ≤24h.
  • Same-day charge-out (D0 @ 18:00).
  • Daily submit cadence with denials worklist.
  • Pay-links or deposits where appropriate.

What you see

  • Charge-lag ≤24h without integrating into your PMS/EHR.
  • DSO reduced by 7–14 days through faster eligibility and clean claims.
  • Earlier cash and fewer write-offs from resolved denials.

30–45 day deltas (examples)

Psychotherapy / telemental

DNA −5–8pp; FRT <4h; touches per booking −30–50%; fill +2–5pp.

Diagnostics (MRI / CT / US)

Fill +3–7pp; DNA −4–8pp; FRT <4h; charge-lag <24h; DSO −7–10 days.

Private GP / specialty

DNA −3–5pp; FRT / TAT −~40%; cost per booking −10–20%.

Simple ROI under the retainer

Back-of-the-envelope per month: Value ≈ extra attended visits × £/visit + admin hours saved × £/hour.

  • Extra attended visits = monthly visits × (Δ no-show + Δ fill).
  • Admin hours saved = monthly visits × (minutes saved per booking ÷ 60).
  • If insurer track active: add cash brought forward from ΔDSO and fewer write-offs from Δdenial%.

Psychotherapy

900 visits; £100/visit; ΔDNA=3pp; ΔFill=1pp; 3 min saved; £20/hr → ~£4.2k/mo.

MRI

600 scans; £250/scan; ΔDNA=4pp; ΔFill=2pp; 2 min saved; £20/hr → ~£9.0k/mo.

Insurer track

At £150k PMI billings/month and ΔDSO 7–10 days → ~£35k–£50k earlier cash.
