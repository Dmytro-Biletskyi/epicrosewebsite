KPI definitions

We track TAT/FRT, DNA%, touches per booking, fill-rate. If insurer track is active we add charge-lag and DSO.

Charge-lag/DSO move via RCM-lite (no integrations): same-day charge-out, pay-links/deposits, eligibility/pre-auth inbox, daily submit, denials worklist.

30–45-day deltas (Example)

Psychotherapy/telemental

DNA −5–8pp; FRT to <4h; touches/booking −30–50%; fill +2–5pp.

Diagnostics (MRI/CT/US)

Fill +3–7pp; DNA −4–8pp; FRT to <4h; charge-lag <24h; DSO −7–10 days.

Private GP/specialty

DNA −3–5pp; TAT −40%; cost/booking −20%.

When a retainer makes sense

A monthly effect ≥ retainer + £2–3k is the threshold. Ideally ≥3× retainer within 90 days.

“Our PMS already has this”

A feature ≠ a managed process with SLA, ownership, A/B, weekly review, waitlist rules. For insurer clinics: PMS features do not replace eligibility/pre-auth inbox with SLA, same-day charge-out, and a denials worklist. Those move charge-lag/DSO.
