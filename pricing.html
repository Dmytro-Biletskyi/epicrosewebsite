Pricing

Start with a KPI-backed pilot, then roll into managed cadence

Pilot — £7.5k (30–45 days)

Includes

  • Core-4 configuration with consent copy and PECR messaging templates.
  • 10–12 SOPs, 12–15 SMS/Email templates and a live dashboard.
  • 45–60 minute team training and weekly WBR cadence.
  • Before/after KPI summary with recommended next steps.

Track selection rule

  • Self-pay track is the default.
  • Insurer/RCM-lite activates automatically if PMI ≥30% or pre-authorisations are present.
  • Eligibility/pre-auth inbox (SLA 24h), same-day charge-out (D0@18:00), daily submit and denials worklist. No EHR, no PHI.

KPI-credit guarantee

0 / 3 targets met

£3,000 credit against the first retainer invoices.

1 / 3 targets met

£1,500 credit against the first retainer invoices.

≥2 / 3 targets met

No credit. Targets: FRT/TAT −40%; DNA −3–5pp; cost per booking −~20%.

Credits are non-cash, exclude pass-through, valid 60 days, and require client SLA adherence (named owners, one training, weekly WBR, data provision).

Scope boundaries

Excludes

  • Any EHR/RIS/PACS integrations or migrations.
  • Clinical workflows, content or protocol changes.
  • Multi-site migrations or large-scale data projects.
  • PHI processing.

Pass-through (capped)

  • Licences / SMS / WhatsApp / voice / transcription capped at £400 per location per month during the pilot without written approval.
  • If partner inbox configuration is required, hours are capped at 24h (fixed or £90–£120/h).

Multi-site & rollout

During the pilot

  • Multi-site pilot (same PMS): +£4.0k per extra location.
  • Different PMS/flows: +£5.0k per extra location.
  • Insurer/RCM-lite, if active: +£1.0k per location.

Post-pilot rollout

  • £3.5k–£5.0k per additional location depending on artefact reuse.
  • Retainer tier scales with message volume and RCM cadence.

Retainer (12-month term, auto-renew)

Lite — £1.2k/mo

  • Core-4 upkeep with SLA monitoring.
  • Monthly WBR, copy tweaks and dashboard upkeep.

Standard — £1.6k/mo

  • All Lite features.
  • A/B cadence, waitlist optimisation, no-show recovery tuning.
  • Charge-day end QA when insurer track active.

Plus — £2.0k+/mo

  • All Standard features.
  • Front-end RCM cadence (eligibility/pre-auth checklists, submission timing, denials worklist).
  • Support for extra locations or higher message volume.

Pilot rolls into a 12-month retainer unless you opt out within 14 days of the final WBR. If monthly effect is below retainer + £2–3k, you can opt out; the pilot KPI-credit still applies.
